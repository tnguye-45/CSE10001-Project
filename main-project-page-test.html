    Inspiration

    This website was built upon the topic of Happiness and World Data to analyze the global well being factors in the world and determine how economic, global, health, and demographic factors all play a part into happiness. Potential users for the website include government officials looking to analyze their own country or others, people looking to move to another country which are seeking out which country is best for their well being, and analysts looking to profile countries and analyze such well being factors.

    The data source was from Kaggle and the links are below. The first data source provide information on happiness score based on the 6 factors of economic production, social support, life expectancy, freedom, absence of corruption, and generosity. The second data source provides info on economical factors such as GDP, imports, and exports. There was also info on ISO codes manually added in order to allow for easy visualization creation. The data sources were chosen because they provied the necessary info on economic, social, and happiness data to analyze the info for countries worldwide.
